View a list of transactions in the Virtual Terminal

To view a list of your transactions in the BluePay Virtual Terminal gateway portal, click on the "Processing" link in the left menu, expand the "Transactions" link, then click on the "Trans. List" link.

You will then be presented with a list of all of your transactions.
